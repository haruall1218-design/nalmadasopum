Our Ethos

Finding Beauty in the Quiet Moments

We believe that the objects we surround ourselves with define the pace of our lives. '날마다소품' is a curated sanctuary for items that encourage a slower, more intentional rhythm.

The Ritual of the Everyday

Luxury isn't found in excess, but in the quality of our attention. A morning tea ritual, the weight of a hand-thrown bowl, the texture of raw linen—these are the small anchors that keep us present in an increasingly fast-paced world.

Our collection is a rejection of the disposable. We seek out objects that grow more beautiful with use, developing a patina that tells the story of your life within your home.

Our Three Pillars

The principles that guide every collaboration and every product we bring into our shop.

Honest Craft

We partner with artisans who honor traditional methods. Every piece carries the visible signature of the human hand, celebrating imperfections as marks of authenticity.

Timeless Material

Natural materials breathe. We prioritize wood, clay, stone, and fiber—earthly elements that connect us back to the environment we inhabit.

Quiet Intent

Functionality is a prerequisite, but soul is our standard. We only select items that serve a purpose while bringing a sense of calm to the space they occupy.

Meet the Hands

Behind every object in our collection is a person who spent years mastering their craft. We don't just sell products; we share the legacy of these makers.

Woodworking

The Wood-shaper's Workshop

Our wooden spoons and trays are crafted from sustainably sourced oak in a small workshop in the outskirts of Seoul, where the grain of the wood dictates the final form.

Ceramics

Earth and Fire

Fired at high temperatures for durability, our ceramic series features glazes inspired by the colors of the Korean landscape—misty mountains, deep seas, and sun-baked earth.
